## Elemen Video Player (Khusus Anime Streaming)

Konteks: Elemen yang **langsung berada di dalam area video (overlay player)** seperti pada platform anime streaming (misalnya Crunchyroll, Bstation). Tidak termasuk UI di luar video (playlist, komentar halaman, dll).

---

### 1. Kontrol Inti (Playback Controls)
- Play  
- Pause  
- Replay  
- Seek / Scrub (drag timeline)  
- Skip Forward (±10 detik atau ±5 detik)  
- Skip Backward (±10 detik atau ±5 detik)  

---

### 2. Timeline & Indikator Progres
- Progress Bar / Seek Bar  
- Playhead (posisi saat ini)  
- Current Time (timestamp berjalan)  
- Duration (total durasi)  
- Buffered Bar (bagian yang sudah dimuat)  
- Hover Preview / Thumbnail Preview  
<!-- - Chapter Markers (Opening / Ending / Part A / Part B)   -->

---

### 3. Kontrol Audio
- Volume Slider  
- Mute / Unmute  

---

### 4. Subtitle (Overlay Visual)
- Subtitle Text (teks terjemahan)  
- Subtitle Background / Outline  
- Subtitle Positioning (biasanya bottom-center)  

---

### 5. Kontrol Tampilan (Display)
- Fullscreen / Exit Fullscreen  
- Theater Mode  
<!-- - Aspect Ratio Toggle (Fit / Fill / Stretch)   -->

---

### 6. Menu Pengaturan (Settings Panel)
- Settings (ikon gear)  
  - Resolution / Quality Selector  
  - Playback Speed  
  - Subtitle Toggle & Language  
  - Audio Track (Sub / Dub)
